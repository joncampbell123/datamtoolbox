Content type:            Filesystem
Filesystem Name:         Linux Extended Filesystem 2/3 filesystem
Filesystem abbreviation: EXT2, EXT3
Filesystem status:       Widely used on all Linux installations and media

Ext2 is considered the official filesystem of Linux. In almost every Linux
distribution you can find the installer will always provide it as a default.

Ext3 is the same as ext2, with the addition of a journalling inode.
